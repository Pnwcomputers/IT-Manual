# ❓ Custom PC - Estimate Questions

To be able to provide a quote for building a custom computer, we have some questions that are crucial to understanding your needs and ensuring we deliver a system that meets or exceeds expectations!

---

## 1. Usage Requirements:

* What will the primary use of the computer be? (e.g., gaming, video editing, software development, general use)
* Do you have any specific software or applications that you’ll be using frequently?
* Will you be doing any high-performance tasks like 3D rendering or running virtual machines?

## 2. Performance Expectations:

* What level of performance are you expecting? (e.g., mid-range, high-end, extreme performance)
* Do you have any preference for processor type? (e.g., Intel, AMD)
* How much RAM do you think you will need?

## 3. Storage Needs:

* How much storage space do you require? (e.g., 500GB, 1TB, 2TB)
* Do you prefer SSD, HDD, or a combination of both?
* Will you need RAID configuration for data redundancy or performance?

## 4. Graphics and Display:

* Do you require a dedicated graphics card, or will integrated graphics suffice?
* What resolution and refresh rate do you plan to use for your monitor?
* Are you planning on using multiple monitors? If so, how many?

## 5. Budget:

* What is your budget range for this build?
* Are there any components where you’re willing to splurge or save?

## 6. Aesthetic Preferences:

* Do you have any preferences for the case design? (e.g., color, windowed side panel, RGB lighting)
* Are noise levels important to you? (e.g., preference for quiet cooling solutions)
* Do you want any specific brands or models for certain components?

## 7. Future-Proofing and Upgradability:

* Do you want the system to be easily upgradable in the future?
* Are you interested in overclocking? If so, should the cooling system be tailored for that?

## 8. Operating System and Software:

* Do you need a specific operating system installed? (e.g., Windows, Windows Professional, Linux, etc)
* Are there any specific software packages or licenses you need included in the build?

## 9. Connectivity and Peripherals:

* How many USB ports do you need? (e.g., for peripherals, external storage, VR)
* Do you require specific wireless capabilities? (e.g., Wi-Fi, Bluetooth)
* Are you looking for any specific peripherals like keyboards, mice, or monitors to be included?

---

These questions will help us gather the necessary information to create a tailored quote that meets your needs and expectations!
