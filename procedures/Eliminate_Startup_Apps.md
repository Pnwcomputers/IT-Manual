# Eliminate Start-up Programs

You can optimize a system, as well as narrow down troublesome apps, by eliminating startup programs.

* You can access the start up program list via the **Task Manager**, or by opening "**Startup Apps**" via System Settings.

Disable pretty much everything in start-up other than:

* **Security Software**
* **Wireless, Audio, Device Apps**
* **Printer/Scanner Software**
* **Online or Local Backup Apps**
* **Synchronization Apps** such as:
    * iCloud Drive
    * Google Drive
    * OneDrive (*If needed)
* **Specialty Device Apps** such as:
    * Garmin GPS App
    * Fitbit
    * Etc.
