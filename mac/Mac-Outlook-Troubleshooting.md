## Last Resort: Reinstallation

7.  **Reinstall Microsoft Office (If Issues Persist):**
    * **How to Uninstall Office:**
        1. Close all Office apps.
        2. Open **Finder** $\rightarrow$ **Applications**.
        3. **Move the Microsoft Office folder** (or individual apps) to the **Trash** and **Empty the Trash**.
        4. Restart your Mac.
    * **How to Reinstall Office:**
        1. Go to the **Microsoft 365 website**.
        2. **Sign in** with your Microsoft account.
        3. Download and install Office.
