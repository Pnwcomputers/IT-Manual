**Rocky Linux** was founded by Gregory Kurtzer, the original creator of CentOS. Like AlmaLinux, its goal is to be a bug-for-bug compatible downstream version of RHEL. It is designed to be the spiritual successor to the original CentOS, ensuring the community always has a free enterprise OS.

* **Based On:** Red Hat Enterprise Linux (RHEL).
* **Primary Focus:** Strict compatibility with RHEL and enterprise reliability.
* **Project Link:** [https://rockylinux.org/](https://rockylinux.org/)
