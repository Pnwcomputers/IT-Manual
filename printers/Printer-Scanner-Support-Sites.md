## Official Manufacturer Support Websites

| Manufacturer | Official Website | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **HP (Hewlett-Packard)** | `https://support.hp.com/drivers` | Use for HP OfficeJet, LaserJet, Envy, DeskJet, and all other HP printers/scanners. |
| **Samsung** | `https://support.hp.com/drivers` | Samsung printers are now **HP-owned**; use the HP driver site. |
| **Brother** | `https://support.brother.com` | Select your model, then choose "Downloads" for drivers, software, and firmware. |
| **Canon** | `https://www.usa.canon.com/support` | Look up your model, then choose "Drivers & Downloads." For PIXMA, imageCLASS, and CanoScan. |
| **Dell** | `https://www.dell.com/support/home` | Enter your service tag or printer model, then choose "Drivers & Downloads." |
| **Epson** | `https://epson.com/Support/Printers` | Choose your product family or search by model for full software packages. |
| **Lexmark** | `https://support.lexmark.com` | Enter your printer model and select "Downloads" for drivers and firmware. |
| **Ricoh / Savin / Lanier** | `https://www.ricoh-usa.com/en/support` | Search your device model and download software or drivers. |
| **Kyocera** | `https://www.kyoceradocumentsolutions.us` | Navigate to Support & Downloads, enter your model. |
| **Xerox** | `https://www.support.xerox.com` | Enter your model number and select your operating system for drivers. |
