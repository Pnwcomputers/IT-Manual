Our commitment to client privacy and data security is paramount whether we are working on your systems in-shop or on-site.

## Access and Disclosure of Client Information

1.  **Passwords and Logins:** Login information for client accounts, computers, and networks will **not be revealed to anyone** in or outside the company.
2.  **No Unauthorized Recording:** **No local records will be kept by ANY field technicians or employees** regarding passwords or private technical information.
3.  **Login Storage:** We do not keep a permanent record of a client's login information unless we are **explicitly asked to do so by the client**.
4.  **Purpose of Access:** Any and all computer and account access will be **solely for conducting repair or support services**.
5.  **Information Confidentiality:** Any obtained personal or technical information will **not be shown or revealed to anyone**.
6.  **Software Integrity:** **No software program(s) owned by the client will be removed or transferred** for any reason from their digital property.

## Data Security and Privacy

1.  **Primary Goal:** Helping to keep your personal data safe against loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, and alteration is our **top priority**.
2.  **Security Measures:** When a system is brought in for service or repair, we utilize **technical, physical, and administrative security measures** that include:
    * **Firewalls:** To help prevent outside unauthorized access.
    * **Data Encryption:** For any information transmitted.
    * **Physical Access Controls:** For where computers are stored and worked on when brought in.

## Non-Disclosure Agreements and Termination

1.  **Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs):** If a company or client has a system with sensitive data or information, **management can and will sign any needed Non-Disclosure/Confidentiality Agreement**. *Field technicians do not have the administrative power to agree to an NDA or CA.*
2.  **Service Termination:** If a client decides to no longer use our services, all copies of any stored information regarding a client and their computer/technical setup will be **returned to the client immediately and removed from our systems permanently**.

## Important Disclaimer

While we do our absolute best to keep our systems and services protected, **the client is ultimately responsible for keeping any/all of their data safe and secure.**
