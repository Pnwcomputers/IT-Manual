# General FAQ

## Service Rates

### Onsite or Remote Support Rates

**Residential Clients**: $100/hour
- 1 hour is usually enough time for most residential jobs!

**Business Clients**: $120/hour (flat rate)

**Trip Fee**: For business or residential clients more than 15 miles from our office, we charge a trip fee calculated based on total distance and travel time.

### In-Shop Service Rates

**Standard Rate**: $80/hour

**Diagnostics**: $40-$80 if extensive testing is required to identify the issue
- Diagnostics completed in under 15 minutes are **FREE**!

**Important**: Please schedule all drop-offs ahead of time to ensure someone is available at the office to check in your system.

## Service Details

### What is the turnaround time for in-shop repair?

**Average turnaround**: 3-5 business days for most jobs
- We prefer to give a longer estimate and deliver sooner rather than the opposite

**With diagnostics**: Add an additional 2-3 days for diagnostic work before repair begins
- We always get customer approval before moving forward with any repair work or service

**Express service**: Some jobs can be completed in as quickly as 24-48 hours, depending on the work needed

## Services We Provide

### Do we buy used computers or parts?

**No**, we do not buy used computers or parts as we cannot resell them with any reliability guarantee.

### What systems do we work on?

We work on:
- Mac/Apple & iOS
- Microsoft Windows & Server OS
- Linux (Desktop & Server)
- Chromebook & Android Systems

### Do we recycle computer parts and electronics?

We do this in a **limited capacity**. 

**Recommendation**: We recommend Earth Friendly Recycling located off of 117th in Vancouver, WA
- **Phone**: 360-433-8575

### Can we help someone who lives outside of the area?

**Yes!** As long as your computer is physically working and you have a good internet connection, we can help anybody, anywhere!
- We use TeamViewer for remote support assistance

### Do we provide data recovery services?

**Yes**, we provide data recovery services for:
- PC, Laptop, and Server drives
- RAID arrays
- Various storage devices

**Pricing**:
- Standard recovery: $100-$200 on average
- Clean-room recovery: Costs vary (quote provided as needed)

**Requirements**: Basic information about the drive issues is needed for a quote

**Note**: We have a clean-room recovery partner for advanced cases. We do not perform data recovery for phones, but our partner can.

### Do we sell or build computers?

**Yes**, we sell and build:
- New "Big Box" computers
- Custom computer systems
- Servers

**What we need**:
- Basic usage information (what the computer will be used for)
- Specific part requests
- Budget

**Warranty**: We provide a **2-year labor guarantee** on all custom-built computers

### Do we provide repair services for printers?

**Physical repairs**: No, we do not physically service computer printers (fix or replace internal parts)

**Services we provide**:
- Installation and setup
- Configuration
- Networking
- General troubleshooting

**3D Printers**: We provide limited service (part repair/replacement, upgrades), but we no longer do custom firmware work

### Do we fix tablets or cell phones?

**Software services**: Yes, we can help set up, configure, and troubleshoot tablets and cellphones

**Hardware repairs**: No, we do not provide physical repair services such as:
- Screen replacement
- Battery replacement
- Charging port repair

**Recommendation**: For hardware repairs, we recommend Simply Tech in Downtown Vancouver, WA
- **Phone**: 360-888-5800

### Do we do wiring service/installation?

**In-wall wiring**: No, we are not low-voltage certified to run any in-wall wiring

**Services we provide**:
- Terminate connections
- Install/replace connection ends
- Set up/install network racks

**Limitations**: We cannot perform in-wall or cable-run wiring work
